The Skill Harbor has just received a significant upgrade, enhancing our ability to navigate the complexities of skill management. With the introduction of the `fathom` command, we can now analyze the operational footprint of our skills, making it easier to optimize our fleet.

Additionally, the **Quartermaster CLI** has been hardened with a more flexible manual editing workflow. You can now use multi-word `EDITOR` commands (like `cursor --wait`) to refine your release manifests. Set the `QUARTERMASTER_EDITOR` environment variable in your `.env.local` to steer the ship with your preferred tools.
